dbo.CDS_StatTempDetail Columns 
ColumnName  Domain  Datatype  NULL  Definition 
Detail_ID    int  NO  The unique system-generated sequential number used to identify the transaction. 
OCR    char(10)  YES  10 digit number that uniquely identifies a player or employee card in the CDS_Card table.  
StatType    char(5)  NO  This indentifies the type of rating. 'Pit' and 'Slot' are the standard defualts, however additional types such as "Bingo", "Keno" or "SportsBook" can be added. 
SlotNumber    int  NO  Slot Number refers to the number of the gaming machine. This number corresponds to the number on the machine's cabinet. 
Location    char(2)  NO  Casinos divide the gaming floor in to different areas. Each area is then divided into small unites called sections. Each section is further divided into small units called locations. Each location is then given a unique identifier. 
Pit    int  NO  The Pit location where the rating was performed. Used for pit ratings. 
TableNumber    int  NO  Unique number representing a specific game table. 
Shift_ID    char(1)  NO  Current cage shift. 'D' = Day, 'G' = Grave, 'S' = Swing. 
GamingDate    date  NO  The gaming date when the transaction was done. 
StartTime    datetime  NO  The date and time when the player began playing the game. 
EndTime    datetime  NO  The date and time when the player finished playing the game. 
TimePlayed    int  NO  The total amount of time in seconds that the player played the game. 
PlayerSkill    tinyint  NO  The rated skill of the player: Soft, Medium, or Hard. These values are setup in OASIS Administrator. 
PlayerSpeed    tinyint  NO  The rated speed the player was playing: Slow, Medium, or Fast. These values are setup in OASIS Administrator. 
Denomination    money  NO  The monetary value a slot machine is setup to use. 
RawAverageBetTimesTime1    money  NO  The manually-entered betting average of the player that was made on one hand. 
RawAverageBetTimesTime2    money  NO  The manually-entered betting average of the player that was made on a second hand. 
RawAverageBetTimesTime3    money  NO  The manually-entered betting average of the player that was made on a third hand. 
MoneyPlay    money  NO  Used by PitBOSS mainly to identifiy the amount of money for game play brought by the player to the table game. 
CashIn    money  NO  The total amount of money that the player used to play the game. 
FrontIn    money  NO  Used by PitBOSS mainly to identifiy the amount of front money the player used at the table game. 
ChipsIn    money  NO  Used by PitBOSS mainly to identifiy the dollar amount of chips brought by the player to the table game. 
CreditIn    money  NO  Used by PitBOSS mainly to identifiy the dollar amount of credit used by the player at the table game. 
CashOut    money  NO  Used to identifiy the total amount of money in hand with which the player walked away from the game. 
JackPot    money  NO  Any Jackpot amount that the player might have won while playing the game. 
TWin    money  NO  Theorical Win. This calculation is based on a number of vairables:
Pit TWin = TmePlayed * Speed * AvgBet * Skill
Speed: Fast, Medium, or Slow.
Slot TWin = Coinin * Par [Hold Percentage] 
PtsEarned    int  NO  The number of Points Earned based on the player's activity. 
PromoPtsEarned    int  NO  Points earned during promotional events. 
DollarsEarned    money  NO  Dollars Earned is based on a points-to-dollars calculation in OASIS Administrator. 
PromoDollarsEarned    money  NO  Promo Dollars Earned is based on a promo points-to-promo dollars calculation in OASIS Administrator. 
CompEarned    money  NO  Comp Earned is a calculated based on the comp calculation in OASIS Administrator. 
PromoPlay    money  NO  Formerly the ExtraBucket1 column. Contains the amount of Promo Dollars played. 
User_ID    int  NO  Unique system-generated ID number given to each casino user (employee). 
RatedByUser_ID    int  NO  The User ID who issued the rating. 
PointCalc_ID    int  NO  Unique ID number used to identify the Points/Comp calculation.
