dbo.CDS_StatDetail 
Logical Entity Name  CDS_STATDETAIL 
Default Table Name  CDS_StatDetail 
Physical Only  NO 
Do Not Generate  False 
Owner  dbo 
Definition  For every indiviual 'Pit' or 'Slot' rating, there is a CDS_StatDetail record. Every time a player pulls out their player card from a slot machince the system creates a record in CDS_StatDetail, based on the player's activity on that machince. For 'Pit' ratings, statdetail records are created when a rating is done in PitBOSS. The information is entered by the pit clerk or the pit boss.

The column was kept for backwards compatibility and may be removed in a future release.
