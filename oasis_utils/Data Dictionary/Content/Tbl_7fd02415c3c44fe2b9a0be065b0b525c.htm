Physical Only  NO 
Do Not Generate  False 
Owner  dbo 
Definition  This is an important table. Because in unnumbered Coupons, every Coupon in the series has the same Validation Number. When the player presents a ticket that gets redeemed, the information is stored in this table. In contrast with CP_Marketing (for numbered Coupons) There is only one entry in CP_Marketing_UN for the entire series.
