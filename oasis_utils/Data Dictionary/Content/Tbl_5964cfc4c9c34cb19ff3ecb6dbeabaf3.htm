dbo.CDS_StatTripDetail Columns 
ColumnName  Domain  Datatype  NULL  Definition 
StatTrip_ID_1    int  NO   
StatType    char(5)  NO  This indentifies the type of rating. 'Pit' and 'Slot' are the standard defualts, however additional types such as "Bingo", "Keno" or "SportsBook" can be added. 
CashIn    money  NO  The total amount of money that the player used to play the game. 
FrontIn    money  NO  Used by PitBOSS mainly to identifiy the amount of front money the player used at the table game. 
MoneyPlay    money  NO  Used by PitBOSS mainly to identifiy the amount of money for game play brought by the player to the table game. 
ChipsIn    money  NO  Used by PitBOSS mainly to identifiy the dollar amount of chips brought by the player to the table game. 
CreditIn    money  NO  Used by PitBOSS mainly to identifiy the dollar amount of credit used by the player at the table game. 
CashOut    money  NO  Used to identifiy the total amount of money in hand with which the player walked away from the game. 
JackPot    money  NO  Any Jackpot amount that the player might have won while playing the game. 
TimePlayed    int  NO  The total amount of time in seconds that the player played the game. 
TWin    money  NO  Theorical Win. This calculation is based on a number of vairables:
Pit TWin = TmePlayed * Speed * AvgBet * Skill
Speed: Fast, Medium, or Slow.
Slot TWin = Coinin * Par [Hold Percentage] 
PtsEarned    int  NO  The number of Points Earned based on the player's activity. 
PromoPtsEarned    int  NO  Points earned during promotional events. 
DollarsEarned    money  NO  Dollars Earned is based on a points-to-dollars calculation in OASIS Administrator. 
PromoDollarsEarned    money  NO  Promo Dollars Earned is based on a promo points-to-promo dollars calculation in OASIS Administrator. 
CompEarned    money  NO  Comp Earned is a calculated based on the comp calculation in OASIS Administrator. 
RawAverageBetTimesTime    money  NO  Used for Pit ratings only. 
PromoPlay    money  NO  Formerly the ExtraBucket1 column. Contains the amount of Promo Dollars played. 
StatTrip_ID    int  NO  The StatTrip_ID is the link to cds_stattripdetail, which contains the detail activity information of the player for that trip.
