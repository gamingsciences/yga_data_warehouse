dbo.BB_REVENUE

Default Table Name  BB_REVENUE 
Physical Only  NO 
Do Not Generate  False 
Owner  dbo 
Definition  Main table to keep track of the electronic and manual revenue of the Slot Machines on the OASIS system, such as: Coins, Bills, Tickets, Coupons, PersonalBanker, and so on, for all periods (CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD).

dbo.BB_REVENUE Columns 
ColumnName  Domain  Datatype  NULL  Definition 
SlotMast_ID    int  NO  A auto-generated system ID assigned by BlackBart when a slot machine is added to the CDS_Slotmast table.  
AuditDate    smalldatetime  NO  Audit date is an accounting reference to a "gaming day" that is in or through the audit process. Gaming day is defined by a period of 24 hours following the Slot Gaming Day Start set in OASIS Administrator. 
Period_ID    tinyint  NO  A unique number to identify each one of the accounting periods: 3 = DTD (Drop-to-date), 4 =CUR (Current), 5 = PTD (Period-to-date), 6 = MTD (Month-to-date), 7 = QTD (Quarter-to-date), 8 = YTD (Year-to-date), and 9 = LTD (Lifetime-to-date). 
Jackpots    numeric(15, 2)  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Jackpots based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
Fills    numeric(15, 2)  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Fills based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecCoinIn    numeric(15, 2)  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Coin In based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecCoinOut    numeric(15, 2)  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Coin Out based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecCoinDrop    numeric(15, 2)  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by Coins calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ScaleDrop    numeric(15, 2)  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Manual Revenue generated by Coins calculated based on Manual entries (e.g. Drops) during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
CoinDropVar    numeric(15, 2)  NO  Amount in Dollars of the difference between the Manual Revenue and the Electronic Revenue generated by Coins. CoinDropVar = ScaleDrop - ElecCoinDrop 
ElecOnes    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by $1 Bills calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecTwos    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by $2 Bills calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecFives    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by $5 Bills calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecTens    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by $10 Bills calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecTwenties    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by $20 Bills calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecFifties    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by $50 Bills calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecHundreds    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by $100 Bills calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ElecBillDrop    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Electronic Revenue generated by Bills calculated based on electronic meters during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ManualOnes    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Manual Revenue generated by $1 Bills calculated based on Manual entries (e.g. Drops) during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ManualTwos    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Manual Revenue generated by $2 Bills calculated based on Manual entries (e.g. Drops) during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ManualFives    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Manual Revenue generated by $5 Bills calculated based on Manual entries (e.g. Drops) during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ManualTens    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Manual Revenue generated by $10 Bills calculated based on Manual entries (e.g. Drops) during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ManualTwenties    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Manual Revenue generated by $20 Bills calculated based on Manual entries (e.g. Drops) during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD).

ManualHundreds    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Manual Revenue generated by $100 Bills calculated based on Manual entries (e.g. Drops) during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
ManualBillDrop    int  NO  Amount in Dollars of the Manual Revenue generated by Bills calculated based on Manual entries (e.g. Drops) during the corresponding period (e.g. CUR, PTD, MTD, QTD, YTD, LTD). 
CashVar    int  NO  Amount in Dollars for the difference between the Manual Revenue and the Electronic Revenue generated by Bills. CashVar = ManualBillDrop - ElecBillDrop. 
DaysOnLine    int  NO  The number of days the slot machine was on-floor with an active status.

TicketOutVar    numeric(15, 2)  NO  Amount in Dollars of the difference between the Manual Ticket Out and the Electronic Ticket Out. Ticket Out Var = Manual Ticket Out - Electronic Ticket Out.
