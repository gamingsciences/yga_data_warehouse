GameName    varchar(20)  NO  Name of the table game. 
SkillFactorHard    real  NO  The hold percentage (amount the casino expects to win) from a hard skill player.

SkillFactorSoft    real  NO  The hold percentage (amount the casino expects to win) from a soft skill player.

GameSpeedMedium    smallint  NO  The average number of decisions a player makes per hour, which is normally affected by how many other players are at the table. 
GameSpeedFast    smallint  NO  The most number of decisions a player makes per hour, which is normally affected by how many other players are at the table. 
DefaultTimePlayed    int  NO  Minimum amount of time that this game must be played before a player is considered for points or comps. 
HardPointCalc_ID    int  NO  Points/Comp calculation for players with a hard skill level.

SoftPointCalc_ID    int  NO  Points/Comp calculation for players with a soft skill level.
