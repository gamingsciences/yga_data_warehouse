Physical Only  NO

Owner  dbo 
Definition  When the the campaigns are downloaded to the Sentinel, a snapshot is taken to identify the active and scheduled campaigns for each EGM. This table holds those snapshots by Campaign and EGM.  
Note  (1:1) link table of current campaign of each sentinel. Scheduled CampaignID is the next campaign after the current campaign.
