Unmerge.StatDetail

Default Table Name  StatDetail 
Physical Only  NO 
Do Not Generate  False 
Owner  Unmerge 
Definition  For every individual 'Pit' or 'Slot' rating, there is a CDS_StatDetail record and those records are moved to this table to preserve the records of a player that was merged with another player. Every time a player removes their player card from a slot machine, the system creates a record in CDS_StatDetail, based on the player's activity on that machince. For 'Pit' ratings, statdetail records are created when a rating is done in PitBOSS. The information is entered by the pit clerk or the pit boss. These records were recorded prior to the merge to facilitate an unmerge.

Meta_ID    AS ([PlayerID])    The Meta ID was previously either a Player ID or a Group ID. Since the IDType column, which allowed a differentiation between the two, will always only support a PlayerID going forward, this column was changed to a computed column where the Meta_ID will always equal the PlayerID value inserted. The column was kept for backwards compatibility and may be removed in a future release.
